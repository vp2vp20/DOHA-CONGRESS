How Do I Submit?

Authors are cordially invited to submit original, unpublished research papers via our centralized online handling system. All submissions must match the official template guidelines and formatting limitations.

  1. Download the Microsoft Word or LaTeX formatting templates directly on our call-for-papers portal.
  2. Ensure your document hides author details to fit the double-blind review criteria.
  3. Submit your final document in PDF standard form through our paper submission engine.
