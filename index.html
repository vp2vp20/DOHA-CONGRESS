About the Congress

This international congress brings together experts to discuss the complex relationship between intellectual property rights and climate change mitigation.

Congress Theme

The Doha Declaration established important flexibilities in intellectual property law for public health. This congress explores whether similar flexibilities could be applied to climate technologies and green innovation.

Key Topics

  • IP barriers to climate technology transfer
  • TRIPS flexibilities for climate change
  • Green patent pools and licensing
  • Innovation models for climate technologies
  • Policy frameworks for technology sharing

Target Audience

  • Intellectual property scholars
  • Climate policy researchers
  • Environmental technology developers
  • International policy makers
  • Legal practitioners in IP and environmental law
